# DM-MauveSushi
📎 Project Name

Marketing for Mauve Sushi – Mokattam

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on designing and implementing a marketing strategy for Mauve Sushi restaurant in Mokattam. It includes a SWOT analysis, seasonal campaigns (like Sham El Nessim), digital presence enhancement, and promotional strategies to increase customer engagement and sales.

📦 Project Scope

Marketing strategy development for Mauve Sushi

5-Week Digital Growth Plan:
This 5-week plan aims to increase brand awareness, engagement, and sales for Mauve Sushi across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Snapchat. The strategy focuses on optimizing current platforms, launching new channels, and implementing structured content pillars supported by paid advertising. Key objectives include:

Growing followers by 30–35%

Achieving a 6–8% engagement rate

Increasing total orders by 25%

The plan integrates:

Organic content and storytelling

Influencer-style short videos

Limited-time offers and seasonal promotions

Retargeting campaigns for measurable results

By the end of 5 weeks, Mauve Sushi will have a stronger digital presence, higher customer interaction, and scalable growth foundations.
